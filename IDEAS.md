# IPTV Player — Feature Ideas

## Continue watching / resume playback
When you stop a movie or episode halfway through, save the position. Next time you select it, offer to resume from where you left off instead of starting over. Probably the most impactful one for a movie/series experience.

## Next episode auto-play
When an episode finishes, automatically queue up the next one (or show a "next episode in 10s" countdown). Saves a lot of back-and-forth navigating through the series browser.

## Favorites / watchlist
A system for pinning specific channels, series, or movies to a quick-access list on the home screen. Useful for mama and mattias who might not want to browse categories every time.

## Recently watched
A small "recent" section on the home screen showing the last 5–10 channels or movies you watched. Makes it fast to get back to something.

## EPG (Electronic Program Guide)
The Xtream API supports EPG data. Show what's currently playing and what's coming up next on each live channel. Would be especially useful for the football profile.

## Search across everything
A global search that works across live channels, series, and movies at once instead of browsing categories individually. Type "breaking bad" and see results from all content types.
